Cornbread

Description

Instant pot recipe for creating delicious home baked cornbread.

Ingredients

  • Creamed sweetcorn
  • Jalapenos
  • Honey
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Cornbread mix

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Grease a pan.
  3. Preheat oven to 375.
  4. Put mixture in pan and bake for 40 minutes.
  5. Finish with a honey glaze once baked.
