Sticky Chicken

Description

Instant pot recipe for creating delicious home cooked sticky chicken. In this recipe we will use apricot jam, but feel free to use any favored jam you like!

Ingredients

  • Apricot jam
  • French onion soup powder mix
  • Chicken thighs or breast - your choice!
  • White rice
  • Broccoli
  • Russian dressing
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix all ingredients together.
  3. Put chicken with all ingredients in a pan.
  4. Cook for 30 minutes.
  5. While chicken is cooking, cook rice and broccoli.
  6. At the end of the 30 minutes, broil chicken for 5 minutes.
