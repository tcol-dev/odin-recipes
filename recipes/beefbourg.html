Beef Bourguignon

Description

Instant pot recipe for creating delicious beef bourguignon.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Chuck roast
  • Mushrooms
  • Golden potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Chicken bone broth
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Put olive oil in your instant pot and set to saute.
  2. When instant pot reads HOT, brown the bacon.
  3. Once bacon is browned, set aside on a plate.
  4. Add diced chuck roast to instant pot and saute until brown.
  5. Once chuck roast is browned, add half a cup of bone broth and scrape instant pot to remove brown bits.
  6. Cancel SAUTE mode.
  7. Add all ingredients to pot and set to PRESSURE mode (high).
  8. Cook for 45 minutes. Allow 10 minutes natural release.
  9. Once pressure cook has finished, set to SAUTE for 5 more minutes.
